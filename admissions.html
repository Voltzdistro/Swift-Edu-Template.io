Admissions

We welcome applications from students who are eager to learn and grow in our supportive academic community. Join us on a journey of discovery and excellence.

Admission Process

1

Submit Application

Complete and submit the admission form with required documents.

2

Assessment

Student assessment and parent interview (if applicable).

3

Review

Application review by our admissions committee.

4

Notification

Admission decision communicated within 5-7 business days.

Admission Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate or passport
  • Previous school transcripts
  • Immunization records
  • Two passport-sized photographs
  • Parent/guardian identification
